Why Foli Client?

  • Built for SMP

    Other modpacks are primarily designed for PvP, Hypixel, or another specific purpose, with survival Minecraft as an afterthought. Foli Client is curated specifically for survival, multiplayer Minecraft first.

  • Easy to use

    Foli Client is an easy way to get started playing modded Minecraft, as it takes care of the hard stuff like installing and configuring mods, and lets you get straight to playing!

  • Improves Performance

    Foli Client allows users to install Sodium or Optifine, providing a substantial performance increase. Foli Client also includes a number of other mods to further optimize the game.

  • Customizable

    Unlike other clients, Foli Client installs itself in the same way that a user would install their own mods. This makes it easy to use Foli Client as a base, and add or remove mods as the user sees fit.
